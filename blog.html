Rawon Recipe

Food Recipi

Rawon is a typical Indonesian food with black sauce made from kluwek.

Ingredients needed 500g beef brisket, 1 beef knee, cut in half 3 fresh kaffir lime leaves, 1 bruised lemongrass stalks, 1 tbsp tamarind juice, 2 green onions, sliced ​​into 1 cm pieces, 1 tbsp Royco Beef Broth, 1 tbsp salt, 3 liters water, 5 tbsp cooking oil. For the fine spices 4 cloves of garlic, 8 red onions 5 keluak fruit, crush and soak the contents in hot water, 4 candlenut grains, roasted, 1 cm turmeric, grilled.

How to make

1. Boil the meat and beef knuckles until half soft. Cut the meat according to taste. Set aside.

2. Add Royco Beef Broth and salt to the meat cooking water.

3. Saute ground spices, galangal, lime leaves and lemongrass until fragrant and cooked.

4. Add the meat pieces to the stir fry, cook until the spices are absorbed.

5. Add the stir-fried meat to the broth. Cook over low heat until the meat is tender.

6. Serve while warm.
